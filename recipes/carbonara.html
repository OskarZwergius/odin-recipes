The best carbonara recipe

This pasta dish is my go to anytime I want something unbelievably delicious that is quick to whip up.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Spaghetti
  • Guanciale
  • Black pepper
  • Parmigiano and picorino

How to make

  1. Start boiling water
  2. Cut guanciale and start frying
  3. Whip up egg yolks and mix with some of the shredded cheese
  4. Cook spaghetti and add to the guanciale while saving some pasta water
  5. Add cheese mixture and use pasta water to make creamy texture
  6. Add cracked black pepper and some more cheese on top
  7. Enjoy!
