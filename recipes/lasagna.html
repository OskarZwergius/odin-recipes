Lasagna Recipe

Here is my centuries old family recipe for a super delicious lasagna that will leave you wanting more and more lasagna

Ingredients

  • Minced Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Canned San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Herbs and Seasoning
  • Lasagna Plates
  • Milk
  • Cheese

Steps to make the delicious lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the lasagna plates
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Uncover and put on broil
  7. Let the lasagna rest before enjoying!
